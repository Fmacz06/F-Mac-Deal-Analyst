ARV Calculator

Enter a Montgomery County address. Comps are pulled from Redfin + Zillow, then each sale is verified against Montgomery County deeds; the report builds on the right. Conservative ARV + 70% Rule.

Deal Inputs

Give just an address → purchase price is taken from Zillow's list price and rehab is assumed $0. Type a purchase and/or rehab and they're trusted as-is (no lookup). Comps: Redfin + Zillow sales, each cross-checked to the county deed — county-recorded price always wins.

Mortgage Inputs

Defaults: 20% down, 7% rate, 30-yr amortization (applied across the board unless changed here). Adjust any field and hit Generate Report to recompute the monthly P&I and cash flow.

Appreciation Inputs

The 10-Year Appreciation Chart blends three signals by weight — market/area 50%, inflation baseline 30%, this property's own history (last sale → ARV) 20%. Defaults: market 4%, inflation 3%. Edit and hit Generate Report.
